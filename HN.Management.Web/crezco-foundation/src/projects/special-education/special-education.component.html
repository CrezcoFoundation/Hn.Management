Special Education

At Crezco, we firmly believe that one size does not fit all when it comes to education. We recognize that there are children within our sphere of influence who require specialized educational resources to thrive.

These resources are not always readily available or affordable, and we are dedicated to addressing this challenge head-on.

Support Services

The Special Education Program is designed to provide targeted financial assistance to individuals with special educational needs, ensuring they receive equitable access to quality education and support services. These funds can be used to cover various expenses related to the developmental and behavioral needs of each student, including specialized learning materials, therapy sessions, and transportation.

Growth For Every Child

Our mission is to inspire and foster both academic and emotional growth for every child and student under our care, irrespective of their unique learning needs; and to empower students and their families, to achieve their full potential.
